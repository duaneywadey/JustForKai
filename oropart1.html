Oro (Part 1)

Could feel the excitement running through my veins A huge excitement on my face, and the joy I couldn’t contain Have I forgotten something? I had to look all over my place Ensuring everything was placed in my suitcase Racing heartbeat as I moved out of the house Kissed my mom goodbye and put on my mask Carefully put on my jacket as I walked towards the terminal Excitement was replaced with nervousness. Unsure if this trip will be a successful one Went inside the bus and listened to some music Tapped on my gallery and observed her priceless beauty “Will she ever like me?” A question that conquered my mind Asked myself, “what if I showed up to her and she realized, Her decisions weren’t right?” Calmed myself as much as I could Finally at the airport! Flying time to Oro is an hour and a half Sat on my seat and unknowingly slept Just woke up all of a sudden The passengers were fixing their hair unkempt Realized that they were preparing for take-off Such a shame that my mind couldn’t concentrate Just landed on the airport of Oro Taxis were everywhere, announcing their rates and promo Picked one and brought me to a place called Oro Messaged her again and asked about the meeting place It was a coffee shop where she sipped coffee And studied for exams which she would ace Asked the taxi to drop me to the nearest gift shop Bought chocolate bars, a bouquet of sunflowers. And even wrote a poem in hopes of getting her in love Hehe! Perhaps this was just my way of expression! What if I actually had a chance, and she said yes to my question?
