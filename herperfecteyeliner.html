Her Perfect Eyeliner

A poem dedicated to the girl with the perfect eyeliner So many boys have their hearts only for her With face as pretty as shades of diamond Perfection on her face never fades, even till dawn She’s a woman of unending aspirations Persevering with full effort for her goals and motivations Special to my heart, that’s what I’m always sure of Rarer than the rarest rubies, her style and character Will she ever read this letter? Could other boys be stronger than me in this battle? I hope for the best, she considers me as her lover Never have I had this love A love as powerful as the brightest stars in the universe
